# Changelog — Beauty Age Glow (Glow edition)

All notable changes to the **Beauty Age Glow** landing page are documented here.
This project follows [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/) (`MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`).

## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-17

### Added
- Initial Beauty Age Glow landing page (Spanish, es-ES) — an alternative design edition of the Beauty Age landing
- New amber / gold "glow & vitalidad" colour scheme and Playfair Display + Sora typography
- New page structure: hero (model + paint splash), stats bar, skin diagnosis list, product highlight (model + jar), alternating botanical ingredient rows (té verde, algas marinas), science explainer cards, 3-step ritual cards, results / before-after, benefits, full-bleed expert banner, featured testimonials, CTA band and final banner
- Built around the new image set (model cutouts, lifestyle photos, botanical and skin-science visuals, eye before/after)
- Shopify-like cart drawer with quantity controls and `localStorage` persistence
- Search modal with live filtering, account modal (login / register demo)
- FAQ accordion, contact form and newsletter with client-side validation and privacy consent
- Cookie banner (Accept / Configure) with `localStorage` consent
- Policy overlays: Privacy, Terms, Delivery, Returns, Cookies
- EU cosmetic claim cautious wording (Regulation (EC) No 1223/2009 and Regulation (EU) No 655/2013)
- SEO meta tags, Open Graph tags, JSON-LD `Product` structured data, canonical and favicon placeholders
- Responsive layout (breakpoints at 1200 / 992 / 768 / 480 px)
